Welcome to the Food Rescue Platform

Connecting food donors with those in need to reduce waste and fight hunger.

Our Mission

We aim to create a sustainable solution to food waste and hunger by connecting food donors with recipients in need. Join us in our mission to rescue food and nourish communities.

How It Works

  1. Donors list available food
  2. Recipients search for donations
  3. Volunteers help with pickup and delivery
  4. Food reaches those in need
