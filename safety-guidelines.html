Safety Guidelines

Ensuring safe food handling and donation practices for all participants.

For Donors

  • Ensure all food items are within their expiration dates.
  • Store perishable items at proper temperatures before donation.
  • Package food securely to prevent contamination during transport.
  • Provide accurate information about ingredients and potential allergens.
  • Clean and sanitize all containers used for food donation.

For Recipients

  • Inspect all received food items for signs of spoilage or damage.
  • Store perishable items at proper temperatures upon receipt.
  • Observe proper food handling and preparation guidelines.
  • Clean and sanitized storage areas for donated food.
  • Dispense food items on a first-in, first-out basis to reduce waste.

For Volunteers

  • Wash hands thoroughly before handling food items.
  • Wear gloves when handling ready-to-eat foods.
  • Use appropriate equipment for transporting food at safe temperatures.
  • Report any safety concerns or issues immediately.
  • Complete food safety training provided by the platform.
